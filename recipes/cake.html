Ice Box Cake

Quick, easy, and no baking required? Yes, please! This easy Ice Box Cake is a delicious dessert to make and share with the kids—and it’s so darn simple to make.

Ingredients

  • Graham Crackers
  • Vanilla Pudding
  • Chocolate Pudding

Steps

  1. Line the baking dish with graham crackers.
  2. Top with vanilla pudding.
  3. Top with more graham crackers.
  4. Top with chocolate pudding. Repeat.
  5. Finish with crushed graham crackers.
